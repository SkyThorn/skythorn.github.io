SkyThorn Team

a little bit about SkyThorn AI Labs

Oguzhan Baser is an expert in AI verification, attestation, and security, specializing in making machine learning systems trustworthy, auditable, and regulation-ready. He advises organizations on cryptographic attestation, secure model execution (e.g., Intel SGX, AMD SEV), and risk mitigation across the AI lifecycle.

He has led enterprise-scale efforts in verifying LLM behavior, deployed hardware-based attestation pipelines, and helped global firms meet compliance with the EU AI Act, NIST RMF, and ISO 42001. His work spans adversarial robustness, bias audits, forensic incident response, and red-teaming of AI systems.

Oguzhan offers consultation from initial AI risk assessment to end-to-end implementation of secure, verifiable pipelines—supporting teams in regulated sectors like healthcare, finance, and defense. His contributions include whitepapers, open-source tools, and hands-on support for AI V&V, fairness assurance, and tamper-proof deployment.
